<ul class="wp-block-list"><li>What&#8217;s a Grand slam offer? -Differentiated, Incomparable, Value-driven</li><li>Objective: To create a valuable offer that is differentiated and unable to be compared to anything else in the marketplace.</li><li><strong>Constituents of a Grand slam offer</strong>&#8211; Getting more customers, getting them to pay more i.e., increase their average purchase value and get them to buy more</li><li>An offer that you present to the marketplace that cannot be compared to any product or service available, combining an attractive proposition, an unmatchable value prop, a premium price, and an unbeatable guarantee with a money model (payment terms)</li><li>an offer that’s so different that you can skip the awkward explanation of why your product is different from everyone else (which if they ask, then they are probably too ignorant to understand the explanation</li><li><strong>Impact of a Grand Slam offer? </strong>&#8211; Increases response rates to advertisements, Increased conversion, Premium prices</li><li><strong>Identify the client&#8217;s pain!</strong> They should be drawn to your solution when they heat the solution to their pain and inversely, what their life would look like without this pain. Articulate the pain a prospect is feeling accurately, they will almost certainly buy what you are offering.</li><li>The client should believe what they are getting (VALUE) is worth more than what they are giving in exchange for it (PRICE)</li><li>The grand slam offer only becomes valuable once the prospect perceives the increase in the likelihood of achievement, senses the decrease in time delay, and perceives the reduction in effort and sacrifice.  </li><li>Talk in terms of things your prospect believes will increase their status!</li><li>Increasing a prospect’s conviction that your offer will actually work for them, will make your offer that much more valuable. Use- messaging, proof</li><li>Try to incorporate short-term, immediate wins for a client. They just need to know they are on the right path and that they made the right decision trusting you and your business.</li><li>Decreasing the effort and sacrifice or the perceived effort and sacrifice can massively boost the appeal of your offer.<br></li><li><strong>Curating your dream offer-</strong><br></li></ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"># Identify dream outcome for the prospect</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"># List all problems that they might face. Think about what happens immediately before and immediately after someone uses your product.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"># List all of those problems as solutions</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"># &nbsp;Figure out different ways to deliver those solutions</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"># Trim those ways down to only the things that were the highest value and lowest cost # Put all bundles together into the ultimate high-value deliverable<strong><br></strong></p>
